About Page

The "Football Manager 2022 Player Wages Dataset" captures up-to-date salary information for 3900 players across the top 6 football leagues as of 2022. Sourced from Football Manager 2022 (FM22), the dataset provides a comprehensive overview of player wages, offering valuable insights into the financial landscape of football. Furthermore, the dataset's potential for expansion in Football Manager 2023 (FM23) suggests a dynamic and evolving resource, pending the discovery of efficient data extraction methods.
